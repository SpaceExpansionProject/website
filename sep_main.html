Space Expansion Project



API-driven, partially open-source game. Manage a space company, conduct research, build rockets, complete missions, and participate in an interplanetary market economy.



Getting-Started Docs

Features


API-Driven Gameplay

Interact with the game exclusively through an API, allowing for automation, integration with other services, and the creation of custom interfaces to manage your space company.

Research and Technologies

Conduct research to unlock new technologies and systems essential for designing and building rockets, and for completing various missions.

Contracts and Missions

Accept and complete missions such as launching payloads into orbit or mining resources from asteroids to earn funds and develop your space company.

Economic System

Participate in a market economy where you can trade mined resources on an interplanetary market, manage your company's finances, and make strategic decisions to increase profits and company growth.

Resource Allocation

Manage various materials and finances strategically to optimize your space missions and ensure the growth of your company.

Collaborative and Competitive Gameplay

Work together with other players on large-scale projects or compete for contracts and market share, adding layers of social interaction and strategic depth.
